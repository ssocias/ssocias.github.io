What is Cassie?

A mobile web app to forecast the outcome of any objective global or personal event.
You make forecasts on all sorts of "casties" (aka events) and earn points for corrrect predictions. Each correct prediction brings you closer to the top of the leaderboard!

Cassie Beta is out now at cassieapp.com.
Check it out and feel free to send us feedback using the "about" link in your profile!

  • Invite friends to participate in personal casties.
  • Compete for points and titles.
  • Share your thoughts on the events you're most passionate about.
